Aaron Nelson

Photographer | @aaronnphoto

Born and raised in Provo, UT I am a self-taught photographer. It first started with my love for the outdoors and getting out to explore the Rocky Mountains that surround my home town. I wanted to share all of these amazing places I've reached on these hikes and climbs. I got my first camera for the purpose of capturing those moments forever and to share them for everyone to enjoy with me.
